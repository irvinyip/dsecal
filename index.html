How the score is calculated?

The DSE score is calculated based on the following factors:

  • Total Score:

    Sum of Core and Elective Subjects:

    5**=8.5, 5*=7, 5=5.5, 4=4, 3=3, 2=2, 1=1 and Unclassified=0

    Reference
  • Weighted Score:

    HKDSE Score with subject weighting is calculated based on the Subject Level Point x Subject Weighting, you should reference to Admission Figures link below to check weightings of your desired subject.

    Current calculation is based on Engineering department weightings.
    Subject weighting example for JS3223 Programme Title: BSc (Hons) Scheme in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence

Total Score Calculation
The total score is the sum of the scores from all subjects.
Faculty Score Calculation
The faculty score is calculated by multiplying each subject's score by its corresponding factor from scale.json and summing the results.
